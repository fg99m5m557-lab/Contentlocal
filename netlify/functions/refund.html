Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to be satisfied with ContentLocal. This policy explains how refunds are handled for our paid subscription plans (Pro and Unlimited).

1. Free Trial

Every user can try the Service with a limited number of free generations before subscribing, so you can evaluate the Service before paying.

2. Subscription Refunds

If you are charged in error, or if you experience a technical issue that prevented you from using the Service during your billing period, contact us within 14 days of the charge and we will review your request for a full or partial refund.

3. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancelling stops future renewals, but we do not provide prorated refunds for the remaining days of a current billing period unless required by law.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at contact@contentlocal.app with your account details and the reason for your request. We aim to respond within 3 business days.
